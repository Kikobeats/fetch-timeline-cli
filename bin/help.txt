Usage:
  $ fetch-timeline [params][options]

Params
  All the params available at
  https://dev.twitter.com/rest/reference/get/statuses/user_timeline

Options
  -l or --limit     Limit the number of tweets to get.

  -s or --save      Save the output in a filename.

Examples
  $ fetch-timeline --user-name=kikobeats --count=200 --limit=3200
